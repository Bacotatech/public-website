El sistema que sustetuye los mandos de garaje potenciando el ahorro, aportando más control y seguridad.

Characteristics

Power supply

You don’t need any external power supply, it connects directly to your garage door with 24v or 12v AC / DC input.

Doors

It works with any door on the market, barrier or gate, and of course, the current controls continue to work.

Simple

A single device with quick and easy installation. Turn your garage door into a smart door.

Opening sensors

You can incorporate two opening sensors to be aware at all times of the status of your accesses by connecting the device to the internet and using the App.
